Freight Management System/Application

One of my first major project when I was innitially contracted under OTP Design-works was to continue the development of a pre-existing freight management application hosted under Zoho Creator. This app utilized Zoho CRM and Books for contact management, and a external freight/trucking management app for additional features not present in Creator. I had created views, functions and features that used FM's (Fluid mobility) API to return crutial data on truck's current location, routing, and status. I was resposible as well for fixing issues present within the apps contact management. This was less a fault of previous work done on the app and more to do with Zoho's Internal Integration.

Point of Sales System

A paint store located in Hamilton, On I created custom HTML/CSS views due to Zoho Creators typically scrubbing any CSS based template languages (Bootstrap, Tailwind, etc), Creator report views with custom sorts and forms that communicate with integrated applications such as:

  2. Zoho CRM

  4. Google Calendar
  5. Mapbox
  6. And much more

Internal Payment Processor

Similar to payment processors like Intelli Pay. Our Payment processor was made to give competitive rates on transactions between our business partners. I was responsible for integrating a new payment option under an existing integration, as well as general debugging/fixes, front-end and back-end. We allowed for saved payment details using tokenizations and additional security measures. The processor used a combination of Zoho Creator, Books, and PHP hosted on Linux.
